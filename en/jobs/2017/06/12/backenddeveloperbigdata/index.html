Backend-Developer for Cloud-based Services


Cognotekt GmbH is the German market leader in automating and improving repetitive business processes consisting of mental activities. We analyse and reproduce them using mathematics and artificial intelligence. And we’re growing, so we need more colleagues to realise our visions. Offer: Your tasks are the design and development of backends for data-driven services for the optimization and automatisation of business processes. Our components are realized in Go and Python (with a core in C or C++). In your role, as a member of a holistic and agile development team, you will • design, implement and test components • integrate the components to create complex systems using continuous integration and continuous deployment We offer • an attractive learning curve in a highly motivated team • competitive salary • an open-ended contract • open-minded, friendly working atmosphere, short decision paths through flat hierarchies You should have • a Bachelor, master or diploma in software engineering or other engineering sciences or similar professional experience in combination with another degree • expert knowledge in at least one modern programming language such as Go or Python • practical experience in software development, preferably in a professional environment • experience working in Linux/Unix-environments • deep knowledge of English, and good German speaking skills • an eagerness to continuously learn and improve • fun in designing and developing complex components Job experience is helpful, but not mandatory. We are a small company with an excellent and very harmonious team. If you have ideas and an opinion and want to ‘get things right’ then you are in the right place. With us, you bring higher mathematics into production for our business customers. We’re looking forward to working with you!
